About me

Hello! I am 25 years old and an enthusiastic, self-taught web developer looking to improve my front-end and back-end skills. I am discovering a rich source of knowledge on the internet and delving into technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on platforms like YouTube and Udemy. My willingness to create incredible interfaces has led me to start many projects on GitHub, where I have applied my skills and tried new ideas. My native language is Portuguese, but I have a strong understanding in English, especially in reading, which allows me to access content and resources in one of the most spoken languages in the tech world. Whether you need the expertise of a qualified freelance consultant or the commitment of a permanent team member for your company, I am here for you and look forward to discussing your project needs. Feel free to contact me at your convenience.

Weather app

I developed a weather app as a hands-on project to learn about React, APIs and Node.js. This app allows users to check weather conditions for various locations in real time. By integrating with a weather API, current weather data is retrieved and presented in a user-friendly format. Users can search for cities or specific locations and instantly receive weather information, such as temperature, humidity, and weather conditions. The project provided valuable experience in working with APIs, handling data, and creating a functional application using Node.js.
